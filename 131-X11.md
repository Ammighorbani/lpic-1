# X11

### In old systems you needed to write your devices in configuration file like your monitor your mouse or keyboard and now we don't have this file on our `OS` and `x11` was for graphical `OS`

---

### Where is X11 files?
**You can find `X11` files in `/etc/X11`**

---

### How to see location of specefic pixel on monitor?
**`xwininfo` Command change your mouse icon to plus icon and if you click on one pixel on your screen you can see that pixel location**

---

### How to see our display information?
**`xdpyinfo` Command show your display information**

---

### How to make limit users can connect to us?
**You can use `xhost -` command to enable access controll and only authorize clients can connect and `xhost +` to disable access controll and whole users can connect and you can only give access to one ip with `xhost +192.168.1.155` command**
